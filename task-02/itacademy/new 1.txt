# Virtual Hosts



<VirtualHost *:8080>
    ServerAdmin root@localhost
    DocumentRoot "/var/www/01-demosite-static/"
    ServerName localhost
    ErrorLog "/var/log/httpd/01-demosite-static-error_log"
    CustomLog "/var/log/httpd/01-demosite-static-access_log" common
</VirtualHost>
